What is TigerFit?

Good Question! TigerFit is a Princeton University fitness app that aims to create a more efficient and centralized way to track workouts, visualize progress, and promote healthy lifestyles on campus. TigerFit aims to simplify the process of working out for new and advanced lifters alike.

What can I do here?

  • Log your workouts and keep notes of them

  • View your workout history

  • View the progression of your exercises and bodyweight

  • Find different exercises for specified muscle groups

  • Receive recommendations weights for certain workouts based on your past history
